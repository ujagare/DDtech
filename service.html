Product Specifications

Parallel Threaded Mechanical Splicing Systems

DD TECH Couplers are Parallel Threaded Mechanical Splicing systems (Mechanical coupler) designed and manufactured for the connection of concrete reinforcement bars ranging from a diameter of 16mm to 40mm. DD TECH Couplers have been manufactured in compliance with IS 16172, ACI 318, BS 8110, ASME Sec III Div. Also, they are designed and manufactured following the quality management system of ISO 9001.

Type 01

Standard (Parallel Threaded) Coupler

Used to connect two rebars of the same diameter with parallel threads for full-strength mechanical connections.

Specifications

MaterialHigh-strength carbon steel
Thread TypeISO Metric / Parallel Thread
Sizes12mm to 40mm (custom available)
Tensile Strength≥ 125% of rebar yield
Surface FinishNatural / Zinc Plated
Type 02

Reducer Coupler

Connects rebars of different diameters while maintaining safe and dependable structural continuity.

Specifications

MaterialHigh-grade steel
Thread TypeDual size internal threads
Sizes25x20, 32x25, 40x32mm
Tensile StrengthMeets/exceeds parent bar
ApplicationColumn-to-slab / beam
Type 03

Position Coupler (Adjustable)

Used when one bar cannot be rotated; allows adjustment during installation in retrofit or congested areas.

Specifications

MaterialAlloy steel
Thread TypeParallel thread + locking
Sizes16mm to 40mm
FeatureNo bar rotation required
ApplicationRetrofit / congested areas
Type 04

MBT Coupler (Mechanical Bolt Type)

Connect reinforcing bars without thread cutting or bar rotation � ideal for repair work, column splicing, piling, and bridge applications.

Specifications

MaterialCarbon steel (Grade 8.8 / 10.9)
Alt. MaterialSS 304/316 (corrosive env.)
MechanismSerrated saddles + lock bolts
Sizes16mm to 40mm
ApplicationRepair / piling / bridges
