About Us

A practical manufacturing and construction partner.

DD TECH began its journey in 2018 under the name DD Enterprises and has since evolved into a trusted name in the construction industry. With a focus on quality, reliability, and innovation, we rebranded as DD TECH to reflect our growing capabilities and long-term vision.

Who We Are

We are a manufacturer of rebar couplers and a construction services provider supporting reinforcement continuity, site efficiency, and dependable project execution through product supply and field-oriented support.

Our Experience

Over the years, we have been involved in major Residential, Commercial, Industrial and Infrastructural projects, giving us rich domain experience and a practical understanding of varied project requirements.

Our Approach

DD TECH's approach and commitment to clients enables us to be predictable. We have always placed quality and customer satisfaction as top priorities, ensuring clients receive value, quality, and certainty while we remain well positioned to support growing infrastructure opportunities in India.

Our Vision

To become a leading and trusted manufacturer of rebar couplers and construction solutions by delivering high-quality products and reliable services with consistent customer trust.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, reliable, and cost-effective construction solutions through superior manufacturing, on-site services, and a strong commitment to quality, safety, excellence, and customer satisfaction.
